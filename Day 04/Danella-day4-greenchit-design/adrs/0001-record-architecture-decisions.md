ADR 0001: Record Architecture Decisions

Status

Accepted (date: 2026-07-01)

Context
Multiple developers contribute to the solution.
Architectural decisions need traceability.
Future maintainers must understand rationale.
Decision

We will document significant architectural decisions using Nygard-style ADRs stored in source control.

Consequences
Easier
Knowledge sharing
Architecture reviews
New team onboarding
Harder
Requires discipline to maintain
Additional documentation effort
Different
Design discussions become auditable
Alternatives Considered
Wiki Pages

Rejected because decisions become difficult to version.

Meeting Notes

Rejected because rationale becomes fragmented.
