# Ticket Triage Tool — PRD

## 1. Persona
- Primary user, context, pain point

## 2. Problem Statement
- What breaks today, who is affected, why now

## 3. Goals & Non-Goals
- Goals (measurable)
- Non-Goals (explicit exclusions)

## 4. Functional Requirements
- FR-1 … FR-5 with acceptance criteria (Given/When/Then)

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Performance, security, observability targets

## 6. Architecture Decision Records
- ADR-001: Framework choice (Next.js) — context, decision, consequences
- ADR-002: Data layer (Prisma + SQLite) — context, decision, consequences
