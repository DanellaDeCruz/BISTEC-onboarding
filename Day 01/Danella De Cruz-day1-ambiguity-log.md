# LearnLanka — Ambiguity Hunt Log

## Brief reference
- Source paragraph (paste verbatim, then highlight ambiguous phrases)

"LearnLanka connects Sri Lankan O/L and A/L students with **vetted tutors** for one-to-one **online sessions**.
Students should be able to find tutors by **subject**, **language**, and **price**; book and **pay**; **rate the tutor afterwards**.
Tutors set their availability and **get paid weekly**. We want it to be **fast**, **secure**, and ready before exam season."
